Venue & Practical Information

Meeting Location

The ISWAT Action Team meeting will be held at the Institute of Theoretical Astrophysics (ITA), University of Oslo, Norway.

Photo credit: University of Oslo

Accommodation

Hotel recommendations will be provided closer to the meeting date. Please check back soon.

Transportation

Oslo has a reliable and well-connected public transport system (metro, tram, bus, and train). Download the Ruter app to plan routes and buy tickets. Tickets are valid across all modes of transport within the city. For most participants, a 7-day Zone 1 ticket will be the most convenient option.

From Oslo Airport (OSL) to the City

The easiest option is the regional train (lines RE10, RE11, or R12) to National Theatre station, which takes approximately 45 minutes. Purchase a Zone 1–4N ticket in the Ruter app. If arriving late at night, the Flytoget express train runs until 00:50, and the Flybussen bus service runs even later.

From the City Center to the Venue

Take the T-bane (metro) lines 4 or 5 in the direction of Sognsvann or Vestli and get off at Blindern station. The ride takes around 15 minutes from the city center.

[!TIP] Google Maps works for Oslo transit, but the Ruter app has more accurate real-time schedules.
